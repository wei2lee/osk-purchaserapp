This Personal Data Protection Policy notice for personal data (“Notice”) is issued to all our valued customers and guests of Ikano Pte Ltd (“[IKEA]”, “us”, “we”, “our” or “ours”), pursuant to the statutory requirements of the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (“PDPA”).

Such third party service providers, agents and/or affiliates or related corporations and/or other third parties would be processing your personal data either on our behalf or otherwise, for one or more of the above-stated Purposes.
